Reacquisition Service

If you or a parent lost Italian citizenship due to naturalization, you may be eligible to reacquire it under Article 17 of Law 91/1992. Choose full-service support or à la carte guidance.

Full Service — What's Included

Step 1: Eligibility Review — 30-minute consultation with legal case analysis and a written roadmap tailored to your situation.

Step 2: Document Preparation — Custom checklist, retrieval of Italian records, guidance for U.S./Canadian naturalization documents, coordination of apostilles and translations, plus residency and ID templates.

Step 3: Application Assembly — We complete the Domanda di Riacquisto form, draft your consular email, set up your Prenot@mi profile, and provide a full submission checklist.

Step 4: Interview Prep & Post-Submission — 30-minute mock interview, walkthrough of your consular appointment, and ongoing support including review of consular feedback, corrections, and follow-up email strategy.
